I am the fish, <br/>
I am the bait, <br/>
I am caught- <br/>
I can't breathe.

I am the bird, <br/>
I am the plane, <br/>
I am hit- <br/>
I can't fly.

I am the paper, <br/>
I am the poem, <br/>
I am burnt- <br/>
I can't be read.

I am the brown, <br/>
I am the clown, <br/>
I am ousted- <br/>
I can't laugh.

I am the love, <br/>
I am the lust, <br/>
I am lost- <br/>
I can't feel.

I am the stone <br/>
I am the bone, <br/>
I am in the grave- <br/>
I can't move.

I am the good, <br/>
I am the bad, <br/>
I am in all- <br/>
I can't die.

Who am I? <br/>
What am I? <br/>
Why am I? <br/>

Still alive...
