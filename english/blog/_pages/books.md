I understood that reading books is a personal experience. The same book would provide different meaning to different people. I've decided not to write book reviews anymore and not setting up your expectation with my personal experience. I've removed all of my existing book reviews. If a book brings out the feeling of "time well spent", you would find it here. This list is compiled from Jan 1, 2021 onwards.

## Memoir
- *Maybe You Should Talk To Someone* - **Lori Gottlieb**
- *When Breath Becomes Air* - **Paul Kalanithi**

## Fiction
- *Daddy* - **Danielle Steel**

## Personal Development
- *Deep Work* - **Cal Newport**
- *The Coaching Habit* - **Michael Bungay Stanier**
- *The Courage To Be Disliked* - **Ichiro Kishimi, Fumitake Koga**

## Business
